The 2023 hike in Gran Canaria 🇪🇸

From getting lost, to life-changing events and horrifying animal noises, all is passing by! For every day, Kaya also made a vlog that you can watch on YouTube (Dutch, but English subtitles). Read it here, enjoy!

In this blog post, I take you along the days of our itinerary. We come across colorful mountains, boiling puddles and a million waterfalls.
